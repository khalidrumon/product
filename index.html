Our Story

Discover the heritage and passion behind Aurora Jewels

Timeless Craftsmanship Since 1985

Founded in New York City by master jeweler Antonio Moretti, Aurora Jewels began as a small atelier dedicated to creating bespoke pieces for discerning clients. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a renowned destination for exceptional jewelry.

Today, under the leadership of Antonio's daughter Sophia Moretti, we continue the tradition of excellence while embracing modern design sensibilities. Our team of artisans combines centuries-old techniques with contemporary innovation to create pieces that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To create heirloom-quality jewelry that tells your unique story through exceptional craftsmanship, ethical sourcing, and personalized service. We believe fine jewelry should be as meaningful as it is beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewelry and services

Do you offer certified diamonds?

Yes, all our diamonds are GIA-certified, ensuring you receive the highest quality stones with verified characteristics.

How does the bespoke process work?

After a private consultation, our designers create sketches/3D renders, then master jewelers hand-craft your piece. Timeline: 4-8 weeks.

Can I preorder upcoming designs?

Absolutely. Pre-orders require a 30% deposit and secure early-access pricing on limited-edition pieces.

What is the warranty on your jewelry?

Every piece carries a lifetime craftsmanship warranty plus complimentary annual cleaning and inspection.

Do you offer international delivery?

Yes. We ship worldwide with fully-insured express delivery. All customs paperwork is handled for you.
